< pre>

The < pre> tag represents the preformatted text continuous spaces and line breaks are respected. This tag also supports all the global and event attributes in HTML.

Example

< !DOCTYPE html>

< html>

< head>

< title>pretag< /title>

< /head>

< body>

The things I do at home:

-Wash dishes

-Clean our house

-Wash Clothes

-Cook Foods

< pre>

The things I do at home:

-Wash dishes

-Clean our house

-Wash Clothes

-Cook Foods

< /pre>

< /body>

< /html>
